About Me

I recently completed my PhD in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Stanford University. I was extremely fortunate to have Prof. Benjamin Van Roy as my PhD advisor, and I also closely collaborated with the inimitable Prof. Richard Zeckhauser. I'm grateful to have received the support of the Robert Bosch Stanford Graduate Fellowship and a grant from SCB10X during my PhD. My research interests lie broadly in the areas of aggregation and learning algorithms.

Previously, I graduated with a B.Tech and an M.Tech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay, where I was awarded an Institute Silver Medal and an Undergraduate Research Award. I was fortunate to have Professors Jayakrishnan Nair and Krishna Jagannathan as my Master's Thesis advisors.

I'm looking for industry roles in India. Please feel free to contact me if you think I would be a good fit for your company.
